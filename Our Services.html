The services we offer

ATL activity

ATL (Above the Line) activities encompass broad marketing strategies that focus on mass communication through traditional media such as television, radio, print, and digital media. As an agency, we provide services in media planning and buying, creating advertising campaigns, branding, and strategic consulting to enhance brand recognition and reach a wider audience.

BTL activity

BTL (Below the Line) activities are marketing strategies that focus on direct communication with consumers. As an agency, we offer various services within BTL activities, including event organization, promotions, tastings, brand activations, and the development of creative campaigns targeting specific consumer groups.
